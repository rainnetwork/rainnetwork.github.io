RAIN NETWORK

RAIN works all through the smart contract leaving your tokens in your control.

Decentralized.

No one party controls the functionality or operation of RAIN. It is all operated individually by each token holder.

Autonomous.

The smart contract that helps RAIN function works solely and independently of the creator. Once deployed, it works as users call upon its functions.

Open Source.

The smart contract code is verified on Etherscan. Anyone who is interested in knowing how the token works or wishes to develop around the project can freely.

RAIN Forecast (Tokenomics)

When it rains, it pours as the old cliche goes.

Our tokenomics are very fair and simple. For every transaction (address to address transfer) 1% of RAIN is taxed and distributed to each staker in proportion to the amount staked. Users must have 1,000 RAIN tokens staked in order to begin receiving rewards. Best of all, the rewards never stop (as long as tokens are being transferred).
